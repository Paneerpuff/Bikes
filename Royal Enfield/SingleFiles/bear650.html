Royal Enfield Bear 650

The Royal Enfield Bear 650 is an upcoming adventure motorcycle designed to take on rugged trails and long-distance touring with ease. Built on the proven 650cc parallel-twin platform, it promises a perfect balance of power and refinement. With a muscular stance, upright ergonomics, long-travel suspension, spoked wheels, and off-road-ready features, the Bear 650 is aimed at riders who crave exploration beyond the tarmac. It’s expected to blend Royal Enfield’s classic DNA with modern adventure-ready performance, making it a bold new contender in the midweight ADV segment.
