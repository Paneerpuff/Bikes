The Royal Enfield Classic 350

The Royal Enfield Classic 350 is a timeless motorcycle that blends vintage charm with modern engineering. Inspired by the post-war era, it features a signature teardrop tank, chrome accents, and classic fenders, all powered by a smooth 349cc single-cylinder engine. The Classic 350 delivers a refined thump, comfortable ride, and balanced handling, making it perfect for both relaxed city rides and long highway cruises. With updated tech like EFI, dual-channel ABS, and a new chassis, it stays true to its retro roots while offering improved reliability and performance. The Classic 350 isn’t just a bike — it’s a statement of timeless style and pure riding pleasure
