The Royal Enfield Classic 650

The Royal Enfield Classic 650 is a stunning evolution of the brand’s timeless legacy, now powered by the refined 648cc parallel-twin engine. Blending vintage elegance with modern performance, it carries forward iconic design elements like the teardrop tank, rounded headlamp, and sweeping fenders, while offering a smooth, torquey ride that’s equally at home on city streets or open highways. With updated features like dual-channel ABS, slipper clutch, and a comfortable riding posture, the Classic 650 delivers the unmistakable Royal Enfield thump with added power, refinement, and touring capability — making it a true modern classic built for today’s riders
